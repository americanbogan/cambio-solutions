About

A practice built on depth, not just credentials.

Cambio Solutions is Hawaiʻi's only consultancy dedicated exclusively to organizational change management — and a practice where the academic foundation and the field experience are both real.

Cambio Solutions is Hawaiʻi's only consultancy dedicated exclusively to organizational change management — built on the conviction that the hardest part of any transformation isn't the technology or the process. It's the people.

Founder Charles Campos brings a PhD in Business, a Master's in Organizational Change Management, and more than a decade of hands-on delivery across government, banking, real estate, higher education, and distribution — spanning more than 15 countries across North America, the Asia-Pacific region, and beyond. Not in an advisory capacity, but embedded in the work: managing stakeholders, supporting leaders, and navigating the human complexity that every transformation produces.

Charles is Prosci-certified and fluent across the field's leading frameworks — ADKAR, Kotter, Lewin, Bridges, and others. But no single model gets applied wholesale. Every organization carries its own culture, its own history with change, its own people. The frameworks inform the thinking. The context shapes the solution.

Every Cambio engagement begins with diagnosis before prescription — a genuine effort to understand your organization's reality before recommending anything. From there, the work is hands-on and sustained: not a strategy handed off at the door, but a practitioner present through execution, ensuring the change actually lands with the people it's meant to reach.

What We Stand For

The principles behind the practice.

People before process.

Technology changes. Processes change. Sustainable transformation is built on people — their understanding, their readiness, and their genuine buy-in. Every decision we make in an engagement reflects that priority.

Honest over comfortable.

We tell clients what they need to hear about their change landscape — including the risks, the gaps, and the hard conversations. Clarity at the start prevents failure at the end.

Context over convention.

No two organizations are alike. Leading frameworks are tools, not scripts. Every strategy we build is shaped by your culture, your people, and the specific challenge you're facing — not a template.

The Work

Built for your situation.

Cambio engagements range from targeted advisory support for leaders navigating early-stage change to full tactical delivery embedded alongside your project team. Some clients need a thought partner for a few weeks. Others need an experienced practitioner on the ground for the duration of a complex, multi-stakeholder transformation. Most fall somewhere in between.

What stays consistent across every engagement is the approach: diagnosis before prescription, strategy built around your reality, and hands-on support through execution — not just the planning stages. The goal is always the same. Change that your people understand, believe in, and can sustain.

If you're facing a transformation — a system implementation, a restructure, a policy change, a merger, or anything else that asks your people to work differently — the right starting point is a conversation. No commitment, no pitch. Just an honest assessment of what you're navigating and how we might help.

Not sure what level of support you need? That's exactly what the discovery call is for. We'll help you figure it out.

Common Engagements

Situations we're brought in for.

Technology & System Implementations

ERP rollouts, platform migrations, new digital tools. The technology works. Getting people to actually use it — and use it well — is where most implementations fall short.

Organizational Restructures

Mergers, reorganizations, leadership changes. Structural decisions get made at the top. The human fallout happens everywhere else. We manage that gap.

Policy & Process Change

New ways of working, compliance requirements, operational shifts. When the change is mandatory but adoption isn't guaranteed, structured OCM is the difference.

Government & Public Sector

Modernization initiatives, digital transformation, workforce transitions. Public sector change carries unique accountability pressures — and a workforce that deserves careful, respectful engagement.

Enterprise Transformation Programs

Large-scale, multi-stakeholder initiatives running across departments or geographies. When the scope is complex, a dedicated OCM practitioner embedded in the program is essential.

Advisory & Early-Stage Support

Not every engagement needs full delivery. Sometimes leaders need an expert thought partner to assess readiness, identify risks, and build a credible change approach before committing to more.
